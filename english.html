Let's get to know ...... We are Latti & co.

We are a family business that attaches great importance to long-term relationships and personal contact.
Thanks to our many years of experience, we can make a difference!
We are very proud of our diverse customer base, from local restaurants
to large international hotel chains who have been placing their orders with us on a weekly basis since our start.
Latti & co is a specialist in the field of artisanal biscuits, chocolate,
(healthy) sweets, nuts and dried fruits.
Our range is of high quality. To guarantee this, we work closely with the
best selected producers in the Netherlands.
We select our producers based on quality, service, freshness, flexibility and a competitive price.
